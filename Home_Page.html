About Us

Welcome to Lifeline Hospital!


With a mission to care for and improve the quality of human life, Lifeline Hospital, Sri Lanka’s leading group of private hospitals & diagnostic facilities, offers some of the country’s most advanced clinical programs.


Founded in 2005, Lifeline Hospital has grown to include multiple facilities strategically located to serve our community effectively. Our network includes state-of-the-art hospitals and diagnostic centers designed to provide exceptional care and meet the diverse needs of our patients.
